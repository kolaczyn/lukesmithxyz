Zelle donation

There is no fee

If you have any bank account in the United States, you probably already have Zelle and don't know it.

It's a protocol built in to your online bank that transfers money for free.

How to use it

  • Log on to your online banking for whatever bank you have.
  • Search for a "transfer money" or "Zelle" section.
  • You may have to confirm your email, but "setup" is automatic.
  • When asked for a recipient, just put in my email address: luke@lukesmith.xyz

Note

If you send a Zelle donation for the podcast with a question or comment, I recommend also sending the question/comment to my email address.

Zelle seems to sometime misplace comments on donations (at least with my bank):
I get the money, but if you have a comment it doesn't always display to me.
